LogBrew is a mobile-first iOS app for indie developers who are tired of context-switching between dashboards. Connect Sentry, Vercel, Railway, PostHog, Linear, and Stripe via OAuth, and every critical event, from a crash in production to a failed payment, appears in a single live feed on your iPhone, with a push notification before you even open the app.

## What LogBrew solves

Most developers monitor five or six services at once. Checking each dashboard manually means slow response times, missed issues, and mental overhead that interrupts your flow. LogBrew registers webhooks with your connected services automatically and streams all events to one place, ranked by severity. You spend less time hunting for problems and more time fixing them.

## Key features

**Real-time push notifications.** LogBrew sends an alert the moment a critical event arrives. Severity filters let you choose between critical-only or all events, so you stay informed without noise.

**Unified live feed.** Every event from every connected project flows into a single chronological feed. Filter by severity, source, or project without leaving the screen.

**Two-way resolve sync.** Resolve an issue in LogBrew and it resolves in the provider too. If a teammate resolves it in Sentry or Linear, LogBrew updates automatically.

**Live log streaming.** Build, deployment, edge, lambda, and exception logs from your connected providers stream into the Logs tab as they are produced.

**Shareable links.** Generate a temporary link to any event and send it to a teammate via Slack, iMessage, or email.

**Saved filters.** Save combinations of severity, source, and project as named filters you can switch between instantly.
